Fire and Allied Perils Takaful

Why I need Fire and Allied Perils Takaful?

Your investment in your property and its contents is protected via fire and allied perils Takaful. In case significant damage caused by fire and allied perils. You can claim the loss from us ensuring your financial stability and business continuity. This is a wider cover which provides protection for accidental physical loss or damage to insured property in addition to the Fire & Allied Perils Takafuls. Factories, power plants, building and office projects including workshop, machinery, equipment, material, goods, fuel, warehouse, premises or offices are covered under this Takaful policy.

Business places, homes and wealth require security from seen and unforeseen losses. These might arise from fires, burglaries, natural calamities and other hazards which are an unavoidable part of everyday life. In this regards, Fire and Allied Perils Takaful provides comprehensive coverage against all risks to make lives and businesses secure. Fire Takaful provides compensation to the covered person or firm in the event of damage to property (i.e. buildings, stock, machinery and other contents). This policy extends customized coverage for diverse industrial sector companies according to their needs.

  • Content Takaful: This policy is specifically designed to cover home and contents (including jewellery & cash) against the risk of fire, earthquake, explosion, lightning, thunderbolt, flood, typhoon, burglary, bursting of pipes, aircraft damage, damage to mirrors etc. The policy may extend to cover personal accident, loss of rent, liabilities under workmen’s compensation act, legal liability to third party property damage and/or bodily damage, personal document etc.
  • Shop Owner Takaful: Shop Owners’ policy covers your shop’s building and contents/stocks against an array of risks including fire, riot & strike damage, malicious damage, explosion, atmospheric disturbances, aircraft damage, impact damage, burglary & house breaking etc. The policy also provides some additional coves like cash in safe/counter/drawer, loss of rent, personal accident and medical expenses.
  • Explosion: Any accidental loss or damage directly caused by explosion.
  • Aircraft damage: Any loss or damage directly caused by aircraft or other aerial devices or articles dropped.
  • Impact damage: Any loss or damage caused by the impact of any road vehicle.
  • Atmospheric Disturbance: Any loss or damage directly caused by storm, cyclone, typhoon, tempest, hurricane, tornado, flood and inundation.
  • Earthquake: Any Loss or damage (including loss or damage by fire) to any of the property insured occasioned or by through or in consequence of earthquake excluding flood overflow etc.
  • Riot and strike damage: Any loss or damage caused by act of any person taking part together with others in any disturbance of public peace or action of any lawful authority in suppressing such disturbance or willful act of any striker or locked out worker.
  • Malicious damage: Any loss or damage by person acting out of personal or private malice.
  • Burglary: Burglary is house breaking accompanied by actual forcible and violent breaking into or out of the premises (e.g home, mill, shop, godown).
  • Electrical Clause 'B': Loss or damage by Fire to the electrical appliances and installation insured of this policy, arising from or occasioned by over-running, excessive pressure, short-circuiting, arcing, self-heating or leakage of electricity, from whatever cause is covered, subject to the terms and conditions of the policy
